Spaghetti with Meatballs

Description

A classic Italian-American dish featuring strands of spaghetti topped with savory meatballs and a rich tomato sauce. Often garnished with grated cheese and fresh herbs.

Contents of Dish

  • Spaghetti (dried or fresh)
  • Meatballs (typically made with ground beef, breadcrumbs, eggs, and seasonings)
  • Tomato Sauce (marinara or a similar tomato-based sauce)
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Parmesan Cheese (for grating)
  • Fresh Herbs (e.g., basil, parsley, oregano)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Optional: Red pepper flakes for heat

Spaghetti with Meatballs Preparation

  1. Prepare the Meatballs: Combine ground meat, breadcrumbs, eggs, seasonings, and any desired aromatics. Form into small balls.
  2. Brown the Meatballs: Heat olive oil in a large pot or skillet and brown the meatballs on all sides. Remove and set aside.
  3. Prepare the Sauce: In the same pot, saut√© chopped onion and garlic until softened. Add tomato sauce, salt, pepper, and any other desired seasonings. Bring to a simmer.
  4. Cook the Meatballs in Sauce: Gently add the browned meatballs back into the simmering tomato sauce. Cover and cook for at least 20-30 minutes, or until the meatballs are cooked through and the sauce has thickened.
  5. Cook the Spaghetti: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add spaghetti and cook according to package directions until al dente.
  6. Assemble and Serve: Drain the spaghetti and place it in bowls. Ladle the meatballs and tomato sauce over the spaghetti. Garnish with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh herbs.
